Gameservers

  1. Tiger Networks

    2021 — Present

    Tiger Networks is a PoliceRP Garry's mod gameserver where I am situated as Lead Developer and community Co-Owner. This server was handed to me in a desolate and bug-ridden state. I compleatly overhauled it to its current standards it can be found at today with a fresh new logo and complete re-design of its in-game features. With a consecutive playerbase of approximatly 25 players and a monthly income of £40 we are slowly rising to the top of the mark when it comes to this GameMode.

  2. Aztec Realms

    2021 — 2022

    Aztec Realms was a SMP minecraft server which combined elements of both the Desert and Jungle which created a unique gameplay experience. This server was a commissioned piece of work in which I created 3 new maps, a logo and fully setup for my client.

  3. Flamin' Gaming

    2021 — 2021

    Flamin' Gaming was a Garry's mod community that focused on minigame gamemodes such as deathrun and prophunt. In it's time we recieved 20 players during peak time, which for a server of its type is good. This server recieved a full setup including its own trailer, website and discord bot.
